INNOVEX 2021

BE Students Final year projects exhibition - DBIT's vision of producing employable engineers who can​ ideate and design solutions for problems relevant to both the society and industry.

What is INNOVEX?

Innovex is a platform for the students to showcase their year long effort and help in encouraging open-ended discussions and deliberations for improving the scope of the project. It also encourages the students to pitch their ideas & solutions in front of the judges from the Industry, Academia and Alumni.

I remember mentioning last year that INNOVEX 2020 was a miracle waiting to happen. I will be more pragmatic this time and state that INNOVEX 2021 is one of the most challenging assignments DBIT has ever taken up. Projects in 2020-21 have been conceptualised, planned and executed in a completely virtual environment. We hadn’t anticipated this. Therefore I can only admire the creativity, forbearance and the resilience of the students and faculty in putting every effort to make the projects take shape and look plausible. I am very proud of the team of faculty Project Co-ordinators who have monitored the progress of the projects and never let the student teams slacken. The efforts put in by them to ensure that INNOVEX 2021 is organized in the most professional manner is praiseworthy. I am equally proud of the faculty guides who tweaked the projects to get meaningful outcomes in the absence of physical models and incomplete validation. Finally, the never say die spirit of our young students and their willingness to stretch themselves and charter unknown territories to redeem their project ideas ( when nature throws lemons at you, make lemonade) is truly admirable. I wish all students and faculty the very best as they present the most important activity of their engineering studies before the world.

Innovex 2021 is here, inspite of the Covid 19 pandemic, inspite of lockdowns!! Yes, this pandemic has taken lives, disrupted economies, left many homeless, penniless and jobless; separated us from loved ones and that which we normally love doing. BUT - it has not been able to lock down Innovex 2021 at DBIT!! True. Innovex this year could not be held the way we would have liked it to be but it is here because of the indomitable spirit of DBITians and I would like to salute that spirit. My congratulations to the Innovex 2021 Project Committee Convener Prof. Cleta Pereira and her dedicated team for launching Innovex 2021. Over the years Innovex, the brainchild of our Principal Dr. Prasanna Nambiar has opened the floodgates of Innovation, Research, Skill Development and dogged Perseverance not only in our final year students but has heightened the aspirations of all the students of the First, Second and Third years too. Knowing that Innovex is a platform that can launch their careers with companies, fire their ambitions in startups and garner support from investors, students leave no stone unturned to give of their best. That is why over the years Innovex has always elicited passion in students and faculty to reach for the stars because the future is quite often shaped by what happens at this event. I commend all the final year students, their guides and mentors and wish that all our BE students sail into the future with flying colours. Congratulations!! Bon Voyage and God Bless.

Since 2017, Innovex has evolved with a brand new set of ideas and design solutions for industrial as well as societal problems. Now in 2021 we've come up with a website to showcase the Final Year Projects during Innovex! This year, the pandemic has engulfed the world but nothing stopped our students and faculty from executing their projects and displaying their hard work. It has been a pleasure working for this exhibition under the guidance of our Principal, Dr. Prasanna Nambiar. I would like to thank the department coordinators - Prof. Sunantha (IT), Prof. Ditty (COMP), Prof. Freda (EXTC) & Prof. Mahesh (MECH) for their continuous support, Prof. Tayyabali for his immense effort into putting up this website and of course all our student volunteers! Wishing all the Final Year students and their guides my very best. With great pleasure and pride, I welcome all our students, faculty and guests to immerse themselves into the broad range of ideas and innovations displayed here by the Final Year Students of DBIT!
